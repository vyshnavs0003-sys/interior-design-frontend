WHAT WE DO

Complete Interior Design Solutions

Interior fit-outs, detailed 3D design and turnkey project management for residential and commercial spaces.

Navora Interiors provides end-to-end interior design services that combine thoughtful planning, premium materials and careful execution. Whether it is a compact apartment or a large commercial space, our team manages every stage from first sketch to final styling so that you move into a finished, ready-to-use interior.

01

Interior Fit Outs

Custom-made interior fit-outs for apartments, villas and commercial units. Our designers plan the complete layout for kitchens, wardrobes, TV units and storage so that every inch of space is used efficiently.

Execution is handled by trained in-house teams who follow detailed drawings and quality checklists. From base cabinets and wall cladding to false ceilings and lighting, all work is coordinated to match the agreed timeline and finish level.

02

Conceptual 3D Design

Before execution begins, we prepare mood boards, 2D layouts and 3D visuals that show how colours, materials and furniture will come together. This helps you understand the design from every angle and request changes with confidence.

The 3D views are matched with practical details such as electrical points, wardrobe internals and kitchen accessories. Once you approve the concept, these drawings become the guide for site work and factory production.

03

Project Management

A dedicated project manager coordinates drawings, approvals, material deliveries and site execution. Regular site visits, photo updates and checklists help keep work on schedule and within the agreed scope.

Quality checks are carried out at each stage - from carcass and shutter finishing to hardware installation and final cleaning. The result is a smooth handover where fittings, lighting and soft furnishings are ready for you on move-in day.

Residential Interiors

Design and execution for apartments, villas and independent homes, including living rooms, kitchens, bedrooms, wardrobes and study areas. Layouts are planned to suit your family's daily routine and storage needs.

Commercial Spaces

Interior solutions for offices, clinics, retail outlets and shared workspaces. We focus on circulation, branding and lighting so that the space looks professional while staying comfortable for staff and visitors.

Turnkey Projects

End-to-end turnkey interiors where design, production, civil work and furnishing are handled by one team. You interact with a single point of contact while we coordinate all vendors and hand over a ready-to-use interior.

Our Interior Design Process

A clear step-by-step process keeps every Navora Interiors project organised, transparent and on schedule.

01

Initial Consultation

Understanding your space, lifestyle, budget and preferred style.

02

Concept & and; 3D Design

Preparing layouts, mood boards and 3D views for your review.

03

Material Finalisation

Confirming finishes, hardware, appliances and soft furnishings.

04

Execution & Supervision

Coordinating site work, factory production and regular quality checks.

05

Completion & Handover

Final cleaning, snag checking and a ready-to-use interior on handover.
